BiHiTo is a hierarchical tokenizer for all-atom biomolecular structures. It maps proteins, RNA, and small molecules into multi-level discrete codebooks inspired by biological assembly hierarchies.

The NHQ module performs biologically structured vector quantization, improving representation quality and OOD generalization. Compared with Bio2Token, BiHiTo reduces RMSD by 25% on RNA3DB and 51% on FastFolding OOD protein multi-conformation reconstruction.
