BioDynaSpec studies spatio-spectral generative modeling for long-horizon protein dynamics. It combines harmonic-guided spectral representations with autoregressive diffusion to generate protein conformational trajectories.

On ATLAS, BioDynaSpec reduces trajectory error by over 60%, demonstrating the effectiveness of spatio-spectral modeling for protein dynamics generation.
