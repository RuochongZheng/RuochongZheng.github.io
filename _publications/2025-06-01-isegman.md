iSegMan is an interactive 3D Gaussian segmentation and manipulation framework. It enables users or agents to control 3D manipulation regions through simple 2D interactions without scene-specific training.

The framework uses epipolar-guided interaction propagation and visibility-based Gaussian voting to map 2D segmentation cues into stable 3D Gaussian regions, then applies a manipulation toolbox for deletion, transformation, copying, and other scene-editing operations.
