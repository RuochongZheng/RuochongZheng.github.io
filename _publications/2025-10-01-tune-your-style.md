Tune-Your-Style studies controllable 3D style transfer for Gaussian Splatting scenes. It introduces Gaussian-neuron style-intensity modeling and a learnable style tuner, allowing users to continuously adjust style strength while preserving scene content.

The method combines cross-view diffusion stylization guidance with two-stage optimization to improve multi-view consistency and stable 3D stylization.
